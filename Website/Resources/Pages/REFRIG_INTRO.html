Introduction to Refrigeration (3 Days)

When you enroll on a Basic Refrigeration & Air Conditioning program at ECAC, you will receive the training and knowledge necessary to understand the operation and function of the major components of the system and how to carry out basic maintenance and fault finding.

The course is designed for tradesmen and technicians whose duties put them in contact with refrigeration and HVAC plants.

The course will cover a wide variety of topics selected to ensure that participants will gain knowledge on a wide variety of systems.
