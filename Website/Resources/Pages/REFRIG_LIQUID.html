Liquid Overfeed Systems (3 Days)

This course is designed for candidates with little or no formal training working with industrial refrigeration systems, but have a good working knowledge or have completed an Introduction to Refrigeration training program.

Liquid overfeed systems are typically used in Ireland in the food processing, brewing and pharmaceuticals industries and typically use Ammonia as a refrigerant. The course covers all of the major components, systems and operations including evaporative condensers and cooling towers.
