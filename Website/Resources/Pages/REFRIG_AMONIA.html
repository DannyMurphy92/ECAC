R717 Ammonia Safety Workshop (1 Day)

Our one day, on-site toolbox talk on R717 (anhydrous ammonia) safety course is designed for all personnel who may come into contact with anhydrous ammonia refrigerant in the workplace. It is designed to make all participants aware of the risks and safety procedures involved in handling R717.

Typical candidates for this course are maintenance and warehouse staff working in cold stores or with plants using R717, as well as emergency response team members.
