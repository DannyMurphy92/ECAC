Intensive F-Gas (5 Days)

This course is aimed at technicians dealing with refrigeration and air-conditioning equipment on a regular basis but due to the nature of their work, do not have exposure to all of the elements necessary to complete the City & Guilds 2079 F-Gas assessment.

The training is a blend of theory, practical and sample assessments over four days, followed by the Fgas assessment on day five.

Candidates will be provided with full course notes. During the practical training days there will be ample opportunity to practice brazing and refrigeration pipe work handling techniques as well as fully installing, commissioning and decommissioning a working piece of Air Conditioning equipment.

Candidates will also be given full back up during the course consisting of both telephone and email support.
