Advanced H.V.A.C. and Process Cooling Systems (3 Days)

For candidates who have completed our Introduction to HVAC systems course, we offer a more advanced program. We will build on your existing knowledge and experience to develop your fault finding and maintenance skills.

Topics covered include HVAC Design, heating and ventilation loads, psychrometrics, absorption systems, VRF Systems, principles of chillers, maintenance, energy efficiency, humidity control and legislation.
