Domestic Heat Pump Service, Maintenance and Diagnostics Training (One Day)

The domestic heat pump market in Ireland is now established and growing. Inevitably there will be a requirement for maintenance and service on existing heat pumps and therefore suppliers and contractors will require a new set of skills to provide your clients with ongoing support on their Heat Pumps.

This course will concentrate on the skills and knowledge required to ensure that the heat pump will be operating at its optimum throughout its life. The course will focus on the heat pump and the most common issues that affect its operation. Specific areas covered include :

  • Principle of operation
  • Annual maintenance requirements for the vapour compression cycle
  • The impact of water side faults on the heat pump
  • Fault code diagnostics
  • Basic electrical component tests (Relays, contactors, capacitors, thermistors etc)
  • Diagnosing a refrigerant loss
  • Diagnosing major component failure. (Fan motors, Compressors, Reversing Valves, Expansion Valves
  • Major Component replacement techniques
  • Leak testing, evacuation and recharging techniques
  • Typical service report completion
  • Water to water heat pump issues
  • The course is based on a generic heat pump and is not specific to any manufacturer or brand
